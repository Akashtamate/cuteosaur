My Cuteosaur · My Love

It’s a quiet night sky.
Somewhere you can rest,
feel safe,
and breathe a little slower.

Made with you in mind. ❤️

For slow moments. For You. For Us

It all started with chess.
Not with grand declarations,
but with patience,
curiosity,
and two minds learning how to wait for each other.
Like Persephone choosing the pomegranate,
not because she was trapped,
but because she wanted to return.

Like Orpheus turning back,
not out of doubt,
but out of love.

Some stories aren’t about loss.
They’re about choosing each other,
again and again.
And sometimes,
magic looks like you
moonwalking by accident.
Some things don’t need reasons.
They just need to exist.
I love you 3000.❤️ — always yours, Sora
